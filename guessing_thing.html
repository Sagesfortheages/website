Rif

Lucena, Spain (1013–1103). Authored the Sefer HaHalachot, foundation of halachic codification.

Rambam

Córdoba → Egypt (1138–1204). Mishneh Torah and Guide for the Perplexed shaped Jewish thought.

Ramban

Girona, Spain (1194–1270). Defender in Barcelona Disputation, mystical and philosophical leader.

Rosh

Germany → Spain (1250–1327). His rulings bridged Ashkenaz and Sepharad traditions.

Tur

Spain (1270–1340). Arba'ah Turim became a cornerstone of halachic organization.
